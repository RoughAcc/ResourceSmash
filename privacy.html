Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2025

At ResourceSmash, I value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how I handle information when you use our website.

Our Privacy Policy

ResourceSmash does not require user authentication or personal details to access the site. It is a free resource hub designed for students preparing for exams.

We believe that educational resources should be freely accessible without barriers. You can browse, download, and use our materials without creating an account or providing personal information.

Our primary goal is to provide valuable academic resources to help students.

Use of Information

While no personal data is collected, ResourceSmash may gather general information like browser type, visit time, and date to improve user experience.

This non-identifiable information helps Me:

  • Understand how users interact with our website
  • Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Enhance the overall functionality of ResourceSmash
  • Make data-driven decisions about future improvements

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Data Security

You are not required to submit personal information to use ResourceSmash. However, if you provide contact details in the feedback section, the developer ensures it will not be misused.

Any contact information voluntarily provided through our feedback forms is:

  • Used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Stored securely and deleted once no longer needed

I value your trust and committed to protecting any information you choose to share with us.

Changes in Privacy Policy

The privacy policy may be updated at any time. Users are encouraged to review it periodically to stay informed.

While I may make changes to our privacy practices, My commitment to protecting your information remains constant. Any significant changes to this policy will be highlighted on our website.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us through the feedback section.
